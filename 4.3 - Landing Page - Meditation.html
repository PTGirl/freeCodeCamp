WHAT IS MINDFULNESS?

Mindfulness is a well-known and popular technique that has been gaining immense popularity in recent years. It can be defined as “paying attention to the present moment, with full awareness”. Mindfulness has many benefits for both individuals and society at large. Many people believe that mindfulness can help improve Stress levels, mental health, relationships, concentration skills, decision making processes and much more! Mindfulness practices include meditation, yoga, tai chi chuan, qigong, among other mind exercise techniques such as deep breathing and relaxation exercises. To learn mindfulness from experts in an online course format is the perfect way to start your journey into this fascinating field!

The emotional and physical benefits of Meditation

  • Gaining a new perspective on stressful situations
  • Building skills to manage your stress
  • Increasing self-awareness
  • Focusing on the present
  • Reducing negative emotions
  • Increasing imagination and creativity
  • Increasing patience and tolerance
  • Lowering resting heart rate
  • Lowering resting blood pressure
  • Improving sleep quality
